About Us

Founded in 2020, West Seattle Movers & Junk Removal is proud to serve the greater West Seattle community as natives deeply rooted in the area with dependable, high-quality moving and junk removal services. As a locally owned and operated company, we understand the value of trust and reliability—our top priorities are delivering exceptional customer service and maintaining a strong work ethic on every job.

Whether you're relocating your home, clearing out space, or handling heavy lifting, our team is committed to making the process smooth, respectful, and stress-free. We believe that how we treat people matters just as much as how well we do the job. That's why our clients keep coming back—and keep referring us to friends and neighbors.

At West Seattle Movers & Junk Removal, we don't just move stuff. We move with purpose, integrity, and a dedication to doing things the right way.
